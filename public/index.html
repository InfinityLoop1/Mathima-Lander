A smart and simple planner designed for students.

Easily track homework, quizzes, and due dates simply and easily with built-in support for your daily schedule.

Features

  • Track homework, quizzes, tests, and projects
  • Organize tasks by name, subject, or due date
  • View tasks in a calendar (WIP) or list format
  • Set due dates based on your schedule instead of exact times (WIP)
  • Get reminders for upcoming deadlines (WIP)
  • Easy and simple schedule builder (WIP)
  • Countdowns for class start and end times (WIP)
